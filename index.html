Our Mission

Code Squad is a nonprofit organization creating a more diverse and inclusive future of technology. We believe this change begins with underrepresented middle school students, our future technologists. As pioneers in a new age of technology, our students develop skills to dream big, lead with confidence and shape a future where technology reflects the makeup of a diverse world. Code Squad accomplishes this by:

  • Sparking curiosity about technology through after school programs, hackathons and relationships with technologists who look like our students.
  • Providing a path to continued STEM success by building confidence, teaching self-promotion and creating inclusive tech communities.
